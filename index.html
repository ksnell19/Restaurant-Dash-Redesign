Miller's

east coast deli

Miller's East Coast Deli has been providing the city of San Francisco with the best food the East Coast has to offer. Dedicating the last 15 years of his life, head chef and owner, Robby Morgenstein, has created a buzz offering the best rueben and other jewish deli favorites to a city with fierce competition in the food and restaurant industry. Located on the busy avenue of Polk Street in the Nob Hill area, Miller's East Coast deli has stood out to be one of the neighbor gems. East Coasters always get a taste of home and West Coasters become lovers of the East Coast taste.

The Breakfast Chief $11
Kosher corned beef and scrambled eggs, with melted cheddar cheese on your choice of toasted bagel, English muffin or challah roll. Served with home fried potatoes.

Pastrami Grilled Sandwich $14
Kosher pastrami grilled with lettuce tomato and red onion. Served with a deli pickle and choice of cole slaw, potato salad or mac salad. Substitute fries or fruit for $2.

House Made Potato Lakes $5.50
Two crispy potato pancakes served with chunky apple sauce and sour cream.
